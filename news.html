The Allman Brothers Show

During the 2017 season, the Redcoats put on a show dedicated to the Allman Brothers Band. Written on the back of an ironing board is forever ingrained into every Redcoat member's memory from that season.

The Homecoming Parade is a yearly event that is looked forward to by the entirity of Dawg Nation. The Redcoat Band leads the parade, blasting Bulldog music as they go.

In one of the classier moves in UGA history, the UGA Redcoats wore silver ribbons to the 2018 National Championships to honor one of the Alabama Flagline members, who passed away due to an inoperable brain tumor.
