Kappa Mu Epsilon Lambda Iota Zeta
The Kappa Mu Chapter is a brotherhood dedicated to the UGA Bands through service, support, leadership and appreciation for music; operating in a respectable and enjoyable manner, with integrity and pride. With a dedicated spirit of unity and cooperation, we are unequivocally committed to become the world standard of excellence for band fraternal organizations. Welcome to the website of the Epsilon Lambda chapter of Phi Mu Alpha Sinfonia at the University of Georgia! Phi Mu Alpha is a fraternity that promotes the advancement of music in America. The Object of this Fraternity shall be for the development of the best and truest fraternal spirit; the mutual welfare and brotherhood of musical students; the advancement of music in America and a loyalty to the Alma Mater. Sigma Alpha Iota (SAI) is an international music fraternity. Music is our main focus; whether it is in composition, education, performance, or appreciation, SAI has it all! SAI harbors a strong interest in our members' abilities to succeed in their musical endeavors, career development, and in giving back to their communities. The Iota Zeta chapter at UGA is in Region VI and Province Tau A.
